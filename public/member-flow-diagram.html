How a Member Check-In Actually Works

Every conversation between a member and Poppie quietly builds the clinical picture — so your clinicians walk into every session already informed.

💬
Member Action
Member Opens the App
Poppie sends a warm, personalized greeting — no login required, no forms to fill out. The conversation feels like a text exchange, not a clinical intake.
😊
Member Action
One-Tap Mood Check-In
Mood rating chips (1–10) appear instantly. The member taps once — no typing needed. This lowers the barrier to daily engagement dramatically.
✨
Poppie AI
Smart Quick-Reply Chips
Poppie reads the conversation and generates 4 personalized one-tap replies — so the member can respond naturally without composing a message from scratch.
🤝
Poppie AI
Conversational Response
Poppie replies in real time — words appearing as it "thinks," just like a live conversation. Responses are warm, brief, and never clinical. Members feel heard, not assessed.
Optional — anytime during the conversation
📸
Member Action
Snap a Meal Photo
Member taps the camera icon and uploads a photo of their meal. No manual logging required.
🥗
Poppie AI
Instant Nutrition Analysis
Poppie reads the image and returns estimated calories, macros, and food categories — automatically saved to the member's food log.
🔒
Automatic · Runs in Background
Silent Pattern Extraction
After each exchange, Poppie quietly reads the conversation, pulls out mood signals and meal mentions, removes any personally identifying details, and writes the structured data to the member's record. The member never sees this step.
🩺
Clinical Outcome
Clinician Walks In Informed
Before the next session, the clinician opens Poppie and gets a pre-built brief — mood trends, dietary patterns, flags, and suggested talking points — all sourced from the member's own check-ins. No intake forms. No manual chart prep.
